My Life and Coding

I’m Kei. I’m a healthcare consultant and web/software developer based in Boston, MA. I started out as a biology major, and worked as a consultant in the healthcare space, advising medical device, healthcare insurance, and pharmaceutical companies.

My first taste of coding came in my biostatistics research lab classes at college, where we used R and Matlab to tackle a variety of research environments and problems. Now I’m creating web apps, and studying to become a full-stack developer. I'm excited to make new projects and change the world, while working with other talented programmers.
